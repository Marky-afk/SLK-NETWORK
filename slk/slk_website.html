Why SLK?
Built different from the ground up
⛏️

Proof of Race Mining

Mine SLK by running physics-based races. Real computational work. No wasteful hashing — every block represents real effort.

🏦

On-Chain Banking

Create Commercial and Reserve banks powered by SLK. Issue currencies, earn fees, offer loans and deposits — all decentralized.

🔐

Multi-Signature Wallets

Require multiple signatures to move funds. 2-of-3, 3-of-5 — perfect for businesses and shared treasuries.

⏰

Time-Locked Transactions

Lock SLK until a future date. Send inheritance, vesting schedules, or savings that unlock automatically.

🗳️

On-Chain Governance

Vote on protocol changes with your SLK balance. Every holder has a voice. Proposals pass by stake-weighted majority.

🌍

True Decentralization

No company controls SLK. libp2p + Kademlia DHT ensures the network survives even if any single node goes offline.
